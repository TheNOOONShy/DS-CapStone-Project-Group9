Staff is doing what they can. Just like everyone else, some believe some do not. Some have a professional ethic some do not. Some care some do not. I do not personally depend on staff to set the example or standard upon which I base my decisions on. So I feel as safe today as I’ve felt every other day. There is no anticipating what another person may decide to do at any given time. I imagine you hope the car next to you follows the rules of the road, however you never know…
