STRESS</br></br>

Confusion reigns supreme,</br></br> 

Making us want to scream;</br></br>

No one knows what to do</br></br>

Causing quite a snafu;</br></br>

Moving here, moving there-</br></br>

Of sudden change beware;</br></br>

Here today, gone the next,</br></br>

Back again - a pretext;</br></br>

Right hand nose not left hand-</br></br>

Both misdirect the band,</br></br>

Leaving all in chaos</br></br>

Because of a virus.
